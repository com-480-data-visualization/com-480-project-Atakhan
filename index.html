What is League of Legends?

League of Legends (LoL) is a competitive, team-based strategy game developed by Riot Games. In each match, two teams of five champions battle to destroy the opposing base. Each player controls a unique character with special abilities, playing a specific role on the map such as top laner, jungler, mid laner, ADC, or support. Known for its fast-paced gameplay, strategic depth, and global esports presence, League of Legends is one of the most popular multiplayer online games in the world.

What is a Beeswarm Graph?

A beeswarm graph is a data visualization that represents the distribution of a set of points along an axis while avoiding overlap between points. Unlike a simple scatter plot, the points are "pushed" sideways to prevent them from overlapping, creating a swarm-like effect.

Usefulness: The beeswarm graph is particularly useful for visualizing density, distribution, and individual values of a dataset while keeping each point visible.

Example of Use: In data science, it is often used to compare the distribution of a variable between several groups (for example, the distribution of scores between two teams).

Advantages:

  • Displays each data point individually
  • Allows for viewing density and dispersion
  • Avoids overlap of points

To Remember: The beeswarm graph is an excellent compromise between a classic scatter plot and a histogram, combining readability and precision.

Champion Explorer: Filters Guide

Everybody has a champion who fits perfectly to them. Why don't you try to find yours?

The Champion Explorer lets you search and filter League of Legends champions using several criteria:

  • Search by name: Type a champion's name to find them instantly.
  • Class: Filter by the champion's main class (e.g., Marksman, Assassin, Tank, etc.).
  • Difficulty: Show champions by their difficulty level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert...)
  • Role: Select the roles played by the champion (Top, Jungle, Middle, Bottom, Support).
  • Range type: Choose between ranged or melee champions.
  • Reset: Click to clear all filters and show all champions.

Combine these filters to explore, compare, and find the champion that matches your playstyle and preferences!
